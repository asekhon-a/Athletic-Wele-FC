About Us

Founded in 2019 by Fanele Gcwabaza and Siyanda Khoza, Athletic Wele FC empowers young boys through football. Initially Fakaza FC, then Ring of Stars FC, finally Athletic Wele FC in 2022.

Statement of Purpose: Empower young boys and give them a future through football.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Inspire young boys to prove themselves on the pitch and in training.

Mission: Overcome every challenge with honor and unity.
